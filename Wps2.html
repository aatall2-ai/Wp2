Getting Started

Launch and Load
1
Launch WPS. Double-click WPS.vbs from the shared drive. The app opens in your browser automatically.
2
Choose Your Module. Select RCA Builder to create a new analysis, Countermeasures to track actions, or Audit to score an RCA.
3
Load an Existing RCA. Click Load to pull an RCA from the shared drive. All fields populate automatically.
4
Start Working. Fill in each DMAIC step. Your work auto-saves to the shared drive.

Associate Workflow

Track and Submit Countermeasures for Review
1
View Your Countermeasures. Open the Countermeasures module. Your assigned RCAs appear with the RCA number and problem description.
2
Work the Action. Click a countermeasure card to expand it. Each card shows the root cause, action, owner, and due date.
3
Submit for Review. Click a pending countermeasure and select Submit for Review. It moves to the manager's queue — shown as blue (Pending Review) until verified.
4
Handle Rejections. If rejected, you will see a banner with the reason. Fix the issue and re-submit for review.
Pending → Pending Review → Verified

Manager Workflow

Verify and Close Out
1
Switch to Manager Mode. Toggle to Manager view in the Countermeasures module. You will see items awaiting verification.
2
Review Pending Items. Click a pending-review countermeasure to review the work. Check that the action actually addresses the root cause.
3
Verify or Reject. Verify to confirm the work is done. Reject with a reason to send it back to the associate.
4
Auto-Archive. Once all countermeasures are verified, the RCA will automatically move to the Archive tab after one week.
ⓘColor-coded sections tell you status at a glance. Red = past due, violet = rejected, yellow = due soon, blue = all pending review, green = all verified.

Audit Checklist

Score an RCA in Minutes
1
Open the Audit Module. Navigate to Audit from the home screen or nav bar.
2
Load or Reference an RCA. Click Load to pull the RCA from the share, or manually enter the RCA number.
3
Answer 22 Questions. Score each question across the DMAIC phases. Questions cover problem definition, analysis quality, countermeasure strength, and control plans.
4
Export Results. Click Export PDF to generate a printable audit scorecard for records.
ⓘThe audit is stateless. Nothing saves. It is a quick scoring tool. Run it, export the PDF, done.

Dashboard

Portfolio View Across All RCAs
1
Open the Dashboard. Click Dashboard from the WPS home screen or the sidebar. It loads every RCA and countermeasure from the shared drive and crunches the numbers in real time.
2
Three Sections. Use the tabs at the top to filter: RCA (completion tracking), Countermeasures (pipeline by status), and Audits (audit coverage). Or view All at once.
R
RCA Section. Summary cards show total RCAs, how many are DMAIC-complete, how many are still in progress, and how many are fully closed (RCA + Audit + CMs all done). Expand the table to see every RCA with its DMAIC phase dots, team lead, KPI, audit status, CM counts, and last modified date.
C
Countermeasure Section. Summary cards show total CMs, open, overdue, due within 7 days, pending review, verified, and rejected. Expand to see the CM Pipeline — every individual countermeasure grouped by status: Past Due first, then Due Soon, Open, Rejected, Pending Review, and Verified. Each card shows the action, owner, due date, relative countdown, pyramid level, and which RCA it came from.
A
Audit Section. Summary cards show total, audited, not audited, and audit rate percentage. Expand to see a table of audited RCAs with team lead, problem, KPI, and audit date.
✓
Fully Closed. An RCA counts as “Fully Closed” when all three conditions are met: all 5 DMAIC phases complete, audit exported, and all countermeasures verified.
!
CM Pipeline is priority-ordered. Past Due items appear first, then Due Soon, then Open. This is intentional — the worst problems surface to the top so leadership can act on them immediately.
ⓘThe Dashboard auto-refreshes whenever you switch to it. No manual reload needed. If you make changes in the Builder or CM Tracker, just flip to the Dashboard and it picks them up.

Countermeasure Tracker Guide

How Countermeasures Get Here

Where They Come From
✓
Automatically from the RCA Builder. When you write countermeasures in the Builder’s Improve step and click Add to CM Tracker, they show up here automatically. That’s it.
2
From the Dashboard. You can also click Track CMs on any RCA in the Dashboard to send it here.

The Three Views

Associate • Manager • Archive
A
Associate View. The default view. Shows all active RCAs with their countermeasures. Associates click a CM to mark it complete (submit for manager review), re-submit after a rejection, or withdraw a pending submission.
M
Manager View. Shows the same RCAs, but clicking a completed CM opens a review modal where you can verify (confirm done) or reject (send back with a reason). Only completed items are actionable here.
H
Archive. RCAs automatically move here once all countermeasures are verified. Archived items are read-only. It's your historical record of completed work.

Associate Workflow

Complete & Submit
1
Find Your Countermeasures. Open the CM Tracker from the WPS home screen. Your RCAs appear as collapsible sections with the RCA reference number. Click a section header to expand it.
2
Review Each Card. Each countermeasure shows as a card with four tiles: Root Cause, Countermeasure, Owner, and Due Date. A status badge on the right tells you where it stands.
3
Use the View Toggles. Each RCA section has three sub-views you can switch between: Countermeasure, Problem Description, and Comments. Use Problem Description to review the original issue. Use Comments to leave notes for your manager.
4
Submit for Review. Click a pending countermeasure card and confirm Submit for Review. It moves to “Pending Review” status and appears in the Manager view.
5
Handle Rejections. If a manager rejects your submission, a violet rejection banner appears on the card with the reason. Fix the issue, then click the card to Re-submit.
6
Withdraw if Needed. Made a mistake? Click a submitted (Pending Review) card and choose Withdraw to pull it back to open/pending status.
Pending → Pending Review → Verified
Pending Review → Rejected → Re-submitted → Verified

Manager Workflow

Verify & Close Out
1
Switch to Manager View. Click the M Manager tab at the top. Items awaiting your review are highlighted.
2
Click a Completed Item. A review modal opens showing the countermeasure details: root cause, action, owner, due date, and pyramid level.
3
Verify. Enter your name in the Verified By field and click ✓ Verify. The CM turns green and is locked. No further changes allowed.
4
Reject. If the work is incomplete or wrong, type a rejection reason (required) and click ✘ Reject. The CM goes back to the associate with a violet banner showing your feedback.
5
Auto-Archive. When every countermeasure on an RCA is verified, the RCA automatically moves to the Archive tab. No manual action required.
ⓘUse the Comments toggle to review associate notes and leave your own feedback before verifying or rejecting.

Quick Tips

Pro Moves
★
Check the Dashboard first. The Dashboard shows a portfolio-wide view of all CMs across all RCAs: total, pending, overdue, verified. Use it to spot problem areas before diving into individual RCAs.
★
Section colors are your early warning system. Yellow = due soon, red = overdue, violet = rejected. Don’t expand every section. Just look at the header colors to find what needs attention.
★
Use comments before rejecting. Managers: leave a comment in the Comments tab explaining what’s needed before rejecting. Associates will see both the rejection banner and your detailed comment.
★
CMs are created in the Builder. There’s no “Add CM” button in the tracker. Write your countermeasures in the Builder’s Improve step and they automatically show up here.
★
Verified = locked. Once a manager verifies a CM, it cannot be un-verified or edited. Verified means done.
